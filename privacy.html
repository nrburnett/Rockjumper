Privacy Policy

Rockjumper Call ID

Effective date: 1 March 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how Rockjumper Call ID handles data.

Data collection and use

Rockjumper Call ID does not collect, transmit, or store personal data through the app itself. Bird identification runs entirely on your device.

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Apple may collect anonymous diagnostic, crash, and usage information in accordance with its own privacy policies when you download or use the app.

Audio files and user-submitted materials

If you choose to contact us by email and voluntarily send audio recordings, images, or other files (for example, bird calls for support or feedback purposes), those materials may be used internally to improve the accuracy, performance, and functionality of the app.

Such materials will not be published, shared publicly, or used for marketing purposes without your explicit permission.

Support communications

If you contact us for support, we may receive your email address and message content. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and provide support.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Updates will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
